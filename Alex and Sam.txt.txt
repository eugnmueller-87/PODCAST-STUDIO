Artificial intelligence is advancing at an unprecedented pace. In 2024 alone, GPT-4o, Claude 3.5, and Gemini Ultra pushed the boundaries of what language models can do. AI is now writing code, passing bar exams, diagnosing diseases, and generating photorealistic images in seconds.
